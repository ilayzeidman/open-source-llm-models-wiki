# Mistral AI Non-Production License (MNPL-0.1)

source_url: https://mistral.ai/licenses/MNPL-0.1.md
fetched: 2026-05-07

## Summary
A custom non-commercial license for Mistral models, including Codestral-22B-v0.1. NOT an OSI-approved open-source license.

## Permitted use (Section 3.2)
- Testing, research, Personal, or evaluation purposes in Non-Production Environments
- "Non-Production Environment" excludes live, real-world conditions, commercial operations, and revenue-generating activities

## Prohibited use (Section 3.2)
- Cannot supply the model or derivatives "in the course of a commercial activity, whether in return for payment or free of charge"
- Covers hosted services, SaaS, cloud instances, any commercial medium

## Redistribution (Section 2.2)
- Must include a copy of the agreement to recipients
- Must retain attribution: "Licensed by Mistral AI under the Mistral AI Non-Production License"

## Modifications & derivatives (Sections 2.1, 2.3, 3.1)
- May "use, copy, modify, and Distribute"
- Derivatives may add their own terms, but cannot restrict third-party recipients' rights under MNPL
- Cannot misrepresent modified versions as official Mistral products without written authorization

## Termination (Sections 7.2–7.3)
- Immediate termination on breach
- Immediate termination if licensee initiates IP litigation against Mistral

## Commercial path
- A separate, paid commercial license is available by contacting Mistral sales

## Practical implication for the wiki research target
- Cannot serve Codestral-22B-v0.1 from a commercial AWS deployment under MNPL alone.
- Sibling Codestral Mamba (Mamba-Codestral-7B-v0.1) is Apache-2.0 and IS commercially deployable.
